A Fair and Secure Access to Everyone

Safe & Quality Projects

Every project will be rigorously verified by our experienced team. The process will include non-negotiable criteria to be respected. For safety reasons, MoonStarter will handle the liquidity lock and the project's different vestings.

Fair Distribution

Depending on how much and how long a partcipant is staking his $MNST tokens, he will have the possibility to partcipate in the upcoming projects on MoonStarter according to a fair calculation based on our Lunar Tier System.

Incubation & Ease for Projects

MoonStarter will make IDOs easier for any project needing to raise funds. Projects will also expand their exposure to potential partcipants and will be able to benefit from our expertise in the crypto environment.
